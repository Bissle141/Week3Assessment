Welcome to American Fork!

More of a city than a Neighborhood, but it'll have to do.

American Fork is yet another city inbetween Provo and Salt Lake City. It's a small mixed bag just like most, but it's got some good places to eat and there is a costco 5 mins from me, so I'm not complaining.
